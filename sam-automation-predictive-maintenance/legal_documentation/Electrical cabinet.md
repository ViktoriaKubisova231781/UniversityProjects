Electrical cabinet 
 
Objects  
Main switch (hoofdschakelaar): central circuit breaker for emergencies (such as short circuits) and maintenance work
Residual current device (RCD) ( aardlekschakelaar):  For personal protection against  electrocution.
Circuit breakers/fuses (automaten/zekeringen): For protection against overload and short circuit per group
DIN rail: On which you mount all components
Terminal blocks (aansluitklemmen): For connecting wires
Neutral and earth rail (Nul-en aardrail): For central connection of neutral and earth wires
Cable entries (kabelinvoeren):  Where the cables enter/exit the cabinet
