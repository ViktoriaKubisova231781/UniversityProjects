About IRIS

IRIS is a cutting-edge plant phenotyping platform developed by the Netherlands Plant Eco-phenotyping Centre (NPEC). We use state-of-the-art computer vision and machine learning to help researchers extract high-quality, quantitative data on plant root architecture.

Our mission is to empower plant scientists by providing intuitive and automated tools for phenotypic data analysis, enhancing both research speed and precision. IRIS is designed to make complex root analysis accessible to all levels of expertise.
